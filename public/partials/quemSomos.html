Established in 1997 by prominent Auckland Real Estate figure Martin Dunn,City Sales Ltd is Auckland's leading apartment specialist.

Flashback to 1991 – the Auckland apartment market is virtually non-existent, a mere 228 apartments in eight buildings. Today City Sales has played a key role in establishing the Auckland apartment market as a great choice for investors, first home buyers and anyone interested in affordable real estate. Currently holding a database of 26,000 apartments across 460 buildings, City Sales Ltd continues to reinforce its position as a leading authority in Auckland apartments.
